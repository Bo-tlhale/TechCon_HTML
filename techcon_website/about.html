Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and innovators. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest international conferences dedicated to exploring emerging technologies, startups, and groundbreaking research.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire innovation, empower collaboration, and drive the future of technology. We bring together thought leaders, developers, entrepreneurs, and academics to share ideas that transform the world.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Ada Lovelace, often considered the first computer programmer, has inspired generations of innovators with her groundbreaking vision of computing's potential.

Alan Turing

Alan Turing’s pioneering work in artificial intelligence and codebreaking has left an indelible mark on modern computing and cybersecurity.

Grace Hopper

Rear Admiral Grace Hopper, known as the “Mother of COBOL,” revolutionized programming languages and remains a symbol of leadership and innovation in technology.
